States Visited

How many states in the U.S. have you visited? My idea of an official state visit has you doing any of the following: exploring the city you're in; spending money on food and entertainment; or interacting with a fair amount of the natives, including family, friends, or strangers.

Let's see how well-traveled you are.

Rules

  • Click on a state that meets the criteria outlined above.
  • Brief layovers do not count. If you never left the airport or hotel, don't click.
  • Do not count business trips where you never left the hotel or office.
  • If you visited a state as a young child and you have no memories of the experience, don't include it.
